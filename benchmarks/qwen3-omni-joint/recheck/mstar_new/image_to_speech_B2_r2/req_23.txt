Here are the key features that identify them:

*   **Translucent Skin:** The skin appears thin and slightly translucent, which is a hallmark of high-quality xiaolongbao. This allows you to see the filling and soup inside.

*   **Filling:** The shape suggests they are filled with a mixture of minced meat (usually pork) and a rich, flavorful gelatinous broth that melts into soup when steamed.
