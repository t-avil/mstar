This is a Caprese salad, also known as "Caprese" in Italian.

It is a classic Italian dish made with fresh ingredients, typically including:
*   **Tomatoes:** Usually ripe, red tomatoes, such as Roma or vine-ripened, sliced.
*   **Mozzarella cheese:** Fresh mozzarella, often in the form of a soft ball (bocconcini) or sliced.
*   **Basil:** Fresh basil leaves are used for flavor and garnish.
*   **Seasonings:** It is typically dressed simply with extra virgin olive oil, salt, and sometimes balsamic glaze.

The vibrant red, white, and green colors of the ingredients are said to represent the colors of the Italian flag, which is why it is so popular in Italy.<|im_end|>
