Based on the visual information you provided, this is a dish of **xiao long bao** (also known as **soup dumplings**).

Here are the key characteristics that identify them:

*   **Steamed Dumplings:** They are cooked by steaming in a bamboo basket, which is the traditional method.
*   **Pleated Wrapper:** The most distinctive feature is the top of the dumpling, which is pinched and pleated to form a small, neat pocket. This is a hallmark of xiao long bao.
*   **Fillings:** The name "soup dumplings" comes from their filling, which consists of minced pork and a gelatinous broth that melts into a savory soup when steamed.
*   **Presentation:** They are typically served in a bamboo steamer, often with a small amount of oil or sauce on the paper lining to prevent sticking and add flavor.
