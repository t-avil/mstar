They are characterized by being made with a thin, translucent rice paper wrapper that is filled with a variety of fresh ingredients. Common fillings include:

*   **Vegetables:** Such as shredded carrots, cucumber, lettuce, and herbs like mint and cilantro.
*   **Protein:** Often includes cooked shrimp or tofu.
*   **Noodles:** Sometimes vermicelli rice noodles are added.
