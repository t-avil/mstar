Based on the visual information, this food is **xiaolongbao** (also known as **soup dumplings** or **shao mai** in some regions).

Here are the key features that identify them:

*   **Pleated, Round Shape:** They have a characteristic pleated top, gathered into a small "bun" or "cap."
*   **Steamed in a Bamboo Basket:** The dumplings are sitting in a traditional bamboo steamer, which is the standard way to cook them.
*   **Thin, Translucent Skin:** The dough appears thin enough to be slightly translucent, which is necessary to contain the soup inside without breaking.
*   **Liquid in the Basket:** The liquid visible in the basket is the soup that has been released from the dumplings during the steaming process.

Xiaolongbao are a famous type of Chinese dumpling, originating from the Jiangsu province. The defining feature is the hot, savory broth contained within the dumpling, which is created by adding a gelatinized meat broth to the filling before cooking.
