Here are the key characteristics that identify it:

*   **Steamed Dumplings:** They are small, round dumplings with a thin, translucent wrapper, which is typical for dumplings steamed in a bamboo basket.
*   **Pleated Top:** The most distinctive feature is the pleated top, which is pinched together to seal the dumpling. This is a classic preparation for Xiaolongbao.
*   **Filling:** The name "soup dumpling" comes from the fact that they are filled with a savory meat filling (usually pork) and a hot, flavorful broth. When steamed, the gelatin in the broth melts into liquid, creating a soup inside the dumpling.
